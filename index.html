Project Smoke Alarm

Introduction

Remote smoke alarms are innovative devices that can alert you to fires from anywhere, providing an extra layer of safety and peace of mind for your home or business. They leverage wireless connectivity to monitor for smoke and quickly notify you, even when you're away.

Working Principle

In this circuit, the MQ2 gas sensor detects the level of smoke in the environment and sends the analog value to the Arduino, which compares the value with the standard value if the value is higher, Arduino sends the signal to the led and the red light will be illuminated and the buzzer will be started otherwise, green led will be illuminated.

Installation and Setup

  • Easy Mounting
      Remote smoke alarms are designed for simple, DIY installation on ceilings or walls
  • Wireless Connectivity
      Connect the alarm to your home's WiFi network using the provided mobile app
  • Customizable Settings
      Adjust sensitivity, volume, and other preferences to suit your specific needs.
  • Integration Options
      Some models offer the ability to link multiple alarms for whole-home coverage.
